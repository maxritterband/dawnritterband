About Dawn

A Life Beautifully Lived

Dawn Ritterband was a woman who filled every room she entered with light. Whether she was laughing with friends, caring deeply for family, or lending a hand to someone who needed it, she showed up fully — present, warm, and real.

She lived her life with an open heart and a generous spirit. Those who knew her will remember her smile, her humor, and her remarkable ability to make people feel seen and loved just as they were.

She is deeply missed, and dearly loved — by her family, her friends, and all who had the gift of knowing her.
